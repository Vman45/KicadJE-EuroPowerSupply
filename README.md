# Base Construction 
The idea is to have a laptop power supply provide the power for the synth, as they are powerful and inexpensive.

## Inputs
19v DC barrel jack.

## Outputs
### Front side
 - Banana +12v
 - Banana +5v
 - Banana GND
 - Banana -12v

### Back side
 - 10 pin doepfer connector
 - 16 pin doepfer connector
 - +/- 12v connector
 - +5 v connector

## Physical
